Welcome to Flood-Watch: A Real-Time Web Based Flood Monitoring System using IoT with Route Information

Flood-Watch is a groundbreaking project aimed at addressing the serious natural calamity of floods in the Philippines. With increasing frequency and intensity of floods due to climate change and urbanization, there is an urgent need for a reliable flood monitoring system. Our system, Flood-Watch, utilizes Internet of Things (IoT) technology and integrates route information to provide accurate and timely flood data. By collecting water level data through IoT-enabled ultrasonic sensors, we offer real-time flood monitoring, helping users make informed decisions and plan safe evacuation routes.

Flood-Watch empowers local government units (LGUs), disaster response teams, and all Filipinos with a reliable and current flood monitoring system. Accessible through a user-friendly web-based interface, the system allows real-time monitoring from any location, enabling prompt action during floods. Our integrated route information feature ranks areas needing immediate attention and provides motorists with alternate routes, minimizing disruptions caused by floods.

Objectives

Flood-Watch aims to assist LGUs and fellow Filipinos in monitoring flood levels to mitigate problems caused by floods, such as stranded individuals, delayed evacuations, and other related challenges.

Specific Objectives

  • Help motorists effectively plan routes during heavy rain or flood situations.
  • Implement a flood monitoring system for LGUs to facilitate immediate evacuation and rescue efforts.
  • Integrate a modern solution to an age-old problem, fostering the path towards smart city development.
